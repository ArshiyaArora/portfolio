AI Interview Mocker

This innovative project leverages the power of the Gemini API to create a personalized interview experience. Built using Next.js, JavaScript, Gemini AI, PostgreSQL, Drizzle, and Clerk, users can generate a custom interview session. During the session, users provide their responses, which are then evaluated. At the end of the interview, a comprehensive review is provided, along with the correct answers, helping users refine their interview skills and boost their confidence.
